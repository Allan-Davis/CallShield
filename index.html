Since CallShield isn't on the Play Store, follow these steps

3
Open & Install Open your Files app, go to Downloads, tap the CallShield APK file and tap Install.
4
Grant Permissions Open CallShield, tap "Grant Permissions & Continue" and accept all permission prompts. This is required for call and SMS blocking to work.
